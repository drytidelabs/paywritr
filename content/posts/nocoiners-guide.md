You've watched from the sidelines. You've rolled your eyes at the laser-eye profile pics. Fair enough. But you're curious, or you wouldn't be reading this.

<!--more-->

## Step 1: Forget the price

Seriously. The price going up or down doesn't matter right now. What matters is understanding *what Bitcoin is* and *why people care*.

Bitcoin is a protocol for transferring value without a middleman. That's it. No bank, no government, no PayPal. Just math and consensus.

## Step 2: Get a wallet

Download **Muun** or **Phoenix** on your phone. They're Lightning wallets — they let you send and receive Bitcoin instantly. Free. Takes 30 seconds.

## Step 3: Get some sats

You don't need to buy a whole Bitcoin ($60k+). Buy $5 worth. That's about 8,000 sats. Use an exchange like **River** or **Strike** — they're simple and don't require a finance degree.

## Step 4: Spend some sats

Pay for this post (you might already have). Buy a sticker from a Lightning-enabled shop. Tip someone on Nostr. The point is to *use* it, not just hold it.

## Step 5: Go deeper (only if you want to)

- Read *The Bitcoin Standard* by Saifedean Ammous
- Listen to *What Bitcoin Did* podcast
- Run your own node (it's easier than you think)

## The honest truth

Bitcoin isn't magic. It's not going to solve all your problems. But it *is* the most important monetary innovation in decades, and understanding it — even a little — puts you ahead of 95% of people.

Start small. Stay curious. Don't trust, verify.
